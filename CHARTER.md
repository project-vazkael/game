# Project Vazkael — Community Charter

This document summarizes the community rules of Project Vazkael.
The full details are available in [docs/GDD.md](docs/GDD.md) — Section 5.

---

## Our vision

> That anyone with the desire to contribute can do so safely,
> that all participants have fun, and are proud to be part of this community.

---

## Who can contribute

Everyone. No application, no selection, no gatekeeping.
Minimum age: **16 years old**.

---

## The two immutable pillars

Only two elements can never be changed by any vote:

1. The medieval / fantasy universe
2. The 3D manga / anime art direction

Everything else belongs to the community.

---

## Content rules

| | Rule |
|---|---|
| ✅ | Stylized manga violence (impact effects, stylized blood) |
| ✅ | AI tools for code, game design, lore |
| ❌ | AI-generated visual assets (sprites, textures, UI, 3D models) |
| ❌ | Characters with childlike proportions |
| ❌ | Sexual or suggestive content |
| ❌ | Realistic gore |
| ❌ | Hateful or discriminatory content |

---

## Financial responsibility

Participation is entirely voluntary. The project cannot be held
responsible for any personal expenses you choose to make.
All core tools required to contribute are free.

---

## Behavior

- Criticism addresses contributions, never people
- Flag problems, propose solutions
- The community moves at its own pace
- Every contribution serves the shared vision

Toxic or malicious behavior results in permanent exclusion by maintainer vote.
